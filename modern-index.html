About Cornerstone One Solutions

Cornerstone One Solutions is a professional staffing and workforce management company dedicated to connecting skilled talent with leading employers across Georgia and surrounding regions.

We specialize in utility staffing, construction labor, industrial workforce solutions, and administrative recruiting designed to keep operations running efficiently and safely.

Our mission is to build reliable teams that drive productivity, safety, and long-term success for both clients and employees.

Our Services

Utility Staffing

Skilled linemen, utility workers, and field crews for power, transmission, and distribution projects.

Construction Staffing

Industrial Workforce
